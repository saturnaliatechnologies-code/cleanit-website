Our Story

CleanIt was born from a simple observation: awareness campaigns about waste segregation are everywhere, but daily action is rare. People know they should segregate, but there's no system to encourage, verify, and reward consistent behavior.

We built CleanIt to bridge this gap. Not as another awareness app, but as a daily habit platform that creates real accountability. Every photo is verified. Every submission counts. Every family becomes part of a movement.

Today, we're growing across Indian cities, one household at a time. Our goal isn't just cleaner binsâ€”it's a fundamental shift in how India handles waste.
