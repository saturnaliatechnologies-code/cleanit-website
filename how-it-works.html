How CleanIt Works

Simple steps to make segregation a daily habit

1

Place Your CleanIt QR

Receive your unique household QR code and place it near your segregation bins. This QR is linked to your home and ensures authentic submissions.

2

Segregate Wet & Dry Waste

Separate your household waste into wet (organic/food waste) and dry (recyclables/non-organic) categories every day.

Build Daily Streaks
