1. About CleanIt

CleanIt is a behavior-based, reward-driven platform designed to encourage daily household waste segregation through verified submissions using QR codes, live camera uploads, location tagging, and time-stamped proof.

2. Eligibility

  • CleanIt is open to users of all ages.
  • Users below 18 years are encouraged to use the app under parental or guardian supervision.
  • By using the App, you confirm that the information provided is accurate and lawful.

3. User Account & Responsibility

  • Each household is assigned a unique CleanIt QR code.
  • Only one valid submission per QR code per day is allowed.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring:
    • The QR code is clearly visible in the photo
    • The photo is taken using the live camera only
    • The submission reflects genuine waste segregation

Any attempt to manipulate, reuse, or falsify submissions may lead to penalties.

4. Submission Rules

To ensure fairness and authenticity, the following rules apply:

  • Gallery uploads are strictly prohibited
  • GPS location and timestamp are mandatory
  • Duplicate or multiple submissions per day are automatically blocked
  • Submissions without a visible QR code will be rejected
